Privacy Policy — Letter Ledger

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Letter Ledger ("the app") is a mobile word game developed by Tinker Apps.

Data We Collect

The app itself does not collect or transmit personal data. However, third-party services integrated into the app may collect data as described below.

Game progress, statistics, scores, and settings are saved locally on your device only and are never sent to any server by Tinker Apps.

The following data is collected by third-party SDKs integrated into the app:

  • Advertising ID (Device ID) — used by Google AdMob to serve and measure ads. AdMob also collects device information, IP address, and approximate location.
  • Purchase confirmation — your in-app purchase is processed through Google Play Billing. The app receives confirmation of a completed purchase solely to unlock the ad-free experience. Tinker Apps does not store or transmit your purchase information.

All data collected by third-party SDKs is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS). While we take reasonable measures to protect data, no method of transmission is completely secure. Tinker Apps does not control how third-party services collect or use this data.

Advertising

The app displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob uses your device's Advertising ID to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. You can reset or delete your Advertising ID at any time in your device's Google settings. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and AdMob's data practices at https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543. Users who purchase the ad-free upgrade will no longer be shown any ads.

In-App Purchases

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase processed through Google Play Billing. Tinker Apps does not receive or store any payment information. All billing data is handled by Google under their terms of service.

Third Parties

The app does not use third-party analytics or tracking services beyond the advertising functionality provided by Google AdMob. Other than Google AdMob and Google Play Billing described above, no other third-party SDKs are included.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings. Data collected by third-party services (such as AdMob) can be managed through your Google account settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data collected about you. Since Tinker Apps does not collect personal data directly, these rights apply to data held by Google. You can manage your data and privacy preferences through your Google account at https://myaccount.google.com.

Children

The app is not specifically directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
